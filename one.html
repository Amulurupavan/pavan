PRABHAS

SALAAR : Part 1 – Ceasefire

2023 ‧ Action/Thriller ‧ 2h 56m

The film was announced in December 2020 with principal photography beginning in January 2021 near Godavarikhani, Telangana. The music is composed by Ravi Basrur, while the cinematography is done by Bhuvan Gowda. It is scheduled for a worldwide theatrical release on 22 December 2023.


PRODUCTION:

Development


In mid August 2020, it was reported that Prabhas and Prashanth Neel were to collaborate for the latter's next directional after KGF: Chapter 2 (2022). The former was reportedly impressed with the latter's debut film Ugramm (2014) and wanted to make a film in the genre.[8] Prashanth, during a media interaction in mid October, confirmed the collaboration between the two.[9] On 2 December, the project was publicly announced by Hombale Films, who are funding the project.[10] Touted to be an action thriller, the film marks the first collaboration between director Prashanth Neel and Prabhas, and the first Telugu venture of Neel. On his role, Prabhas stated: "My character is extremely violent, so this is something I haven’t really done before."[11] A few reports claimed that the film is a remake of Neel's debut film Ugramm, however, Neel clarified that Salaar is not a remake but an original story written for Prabhas.

Casting

Actress Shruti Haasan was cast in a leading role in January 2021.[14] Kannada actor Madhu Guruswamy is set to play a pivotal role.[15] In March, actress Easwari Rao was signed to play Prabhas' mother.[16] Later in August, Jagapathi Babu joined the cast, whose character was revealed as Rajamanaar.[17] In October 2021, Malayalam actor Prithviraj Sukumaran entered talks to play a crucial role in the film.[18] In March 2022, Prabhas said that Prithviraj is part of Salaar during a promotional event for his film Radhe Shyam.[19][20] Later in June, Prithviraj stated that he gave a nod to the script but was trying to adjust the dates to work in the film.[21] Later in October 2022, Prithiviraj's role was confirmed and the film marks his return to Telugu cinema after 12 years, following his appearance in Police Police (2010).[22] Sriya Reddy is also cast in a key role.
